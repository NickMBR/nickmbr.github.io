Descrição:

Cobertura is my first released map for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Focused on the Demolition Gamemode, Cobertura is placed at the rooftop of a modern building. My goal with this map was give an open, colorful architecture feeling to CS:GO while allowing players to use any weapon they choose, as short to long range lines of fire are possible on this map. Roofs of the Main Elevator and the Art Gallery Buildings are acessible giving an extra advantage.

Cobetura é meu primeiro mapa lançado para o Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Com foco no modo de jogo Demolição, Cobertura é localizado no topo de um prédio moderno.
